Q18. The ratio of boys to girls is 4 : 3.
If 7 more girls join, the ratio becomes 4 : 4.
Find the original number of girls.

A) 21
B) 24
C) 28
D) 14

👉 Girls = 3x
New ratio → 4x : (3x + 7) = 1 : 1
So, 4x = 3x + 7 → x = 7
Girls = 3x = 21

✅ Ans: A) 21
